Competition Overview

The Fashion Design Challenge is an exciting competition that seeks to discover the best talents in Abuja. This competition invites participants to create original craftmanship that reflect creativity, cultural heritage, and modern trends.

Objectives

  • To showcase the creativity of indigenous talent in Abuja.
  • To provide a platform for upcoming talents to gain recognition.
  • To promote cultural heritage through fashion.

Competition Rules

  • Participants must submit their Craft or Designs by the deadline.
  • All Craft or Designs must be original and created by the participant.
  • Designs should reflect cultural heritage combined with modern trends.
  • Participants may submit up to three different designs.
  • All submissions must include a brief description of the design and the inspiration behind it.

Prizes

  • 1st Place: ₦500,000, a Desing feature in a top magazine, and a sponsored fashion show.
  • 2nd Place: ₦250,000 and a Desing feature in a top magazine.
  • 3rd Place: ₦100,000 and a Desing feature in a top magazine.

Submission Guidelines

Participants must submit their designs in the form of sketches, photographs, or digital illustrations. Each submission should be accompanied by a description of the design, materials used, and the inspiration behind the piece. Submissions must be made through the competition portal by uploading the files in JPEG, PNG, or PDF format.

How to Participate

To participate in the Fashion Design Challenge, follow these steps:

  1. Register for the competition by clicking the "Participate Now" button below.
  2. Complete the registration form with your personal details and a brief bio.
  3. Upload your design submissions following the guidelines provided.
  4. Submit your entries before the deadline of September 30, 2024.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who can participate in this competition?

This competition is open to all indigenous fashion designers in Abuja, FCT, Nigeria.

Can I submit multiple designs?

Yes, each participant can submit up to three different designs.

What are the judging criteria?

Designs will be judged based on creativity, originality, cultural representation, and overall presentation.

How will winners be announced?

Winners will be announced on our website and social media channels. They will also be contacted directly via email.
